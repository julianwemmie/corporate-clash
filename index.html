Welcome to Corporate Clash

Build your corporate empire, hire employees, and clash with rival players in real time. Grow your business faster than the competition — or take theirs by force.

How to Play

Build — Hover over an empty tile and press [1-4] to place a building.

Hire — Hover over a building and press [1-4] to hire employees. They generate revenue every second.

Attack — Press [A] to pick a target, select troops, then [Space] to launch.

Defend — Build a law firm and hire lawyers to protect your empire from attacks.

Ready?

That's all you need to know. Build fast, hire smart, and crush the competition. Good luck!
